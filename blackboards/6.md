# Blackboard 6 — PDM Gap Formula Correction

## Setup

ordering-equivalence paper, Section 3.4 (PDM Harmonic Oscillator).
$f(q) = 1 + \alpha q^2$, units $\hbar = \omega = m = 1$.

## Layer 3 scalar difference (Section 3.3 — CORRECT)

$$\delta V = V_{\rm HD} - V_{\rm LR} = \frac{\hbar^2}{8}f'' - \frac{\hbar^2}{32}\frac{(f')^2}{f}$$

For $f = 1+\alpha q^2$: $f''=2\alpha$, $f'=2\alpha q$, $(f')^2/f \approx 4\alpha^2 q^2$ to $O(\alpha^2)$:

$$\delta V = \frac{\alpha}{4} - \frac{\alpha^2 q^2}{8} + O(\alpha^3) \quad \text{(confirmed by SymPy)}$$

## First-order PT gap correction — ERROR FOUND

Paper (Section 3.4) claims:
$$\delta(E_n - E_0) = -\frac{\hbar\omega\,\alpha^2\,n}{16} + O(\alpha^3)$$

**Correct derivation:**

$\langle n | \delta V | n \rangle = \tfrac{\alpha}{4} - \tfrac{\alpha^2}{8}\langle n|q^2|n\rangle = \tfrac{\alpha}{4} - \tfrac{\alpha^2(2n+1)}{16}$

$\delta(E_n - E_0) = \langle n|\delta V|n\rangle - \langle 0|\delta V|0\rangle = -\tfrac{\alpha^2}{16}(2n+1) + \tfrac{\alpha^2}{16} = -\tfrac{\alpha^2 \cdot 2n}{16}$

$$\boxed{\delta(E_n - E_0) = -\frac{\hbar\omega\,\alpha^2\,n}{8} + O(\alpha^3)}$$

**Factor of 2 error in paper.** Origin: $\langle n|q^2|n\rangle = n+1/2$, not $n/2$.
