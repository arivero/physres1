# Critique: rg-fundamental satellite

## Severity-2 (significant gaps)

**S2.1 "RG is definitional" overclaims (RG-H1.17, line 703)**
- Claim: RG is "a structural requirement for any definition of a continuum theory via composition of local pieces"
- Reality: The calculus micro-model (Section 3) shows some limit procedures require subtraction; does not prove RG invariance is the unique or correct compatibility condition
- The analogy (derivative = counterterm subtraction) is evocative but not a derivation
- Cross-paper overclaiming pattern: "evocative analogy" presented as "structural requirement"
- Fix: "RG invariance can be *read as* a compatibility condition" (not "is the compatibility condition")

**S2.2 Two-layer QFT claim imprecise (RG-H1.18, line 710)**
- Claim: "Layer 1 constrains structure, Layer 2 constrains content" with "logically independent" layers
- Reality: Layer 1 (per-mode quantization) already depends on the interaction (potential in the Hamiltonian); Layer 2 (assembly/renormalization) constrains admissible interactions
- Logical independence is stated without proof
- Fix: State the two-layer decomposition as a *heuristic organizational principle*, not a theorem

## Algebra checks

**RG-D1.1 (loop integral):** $I(E;\Lambda) = -\frac{m}{2\pi\hbar^2}[\ln(\Lambda^2/k^2) + i\pi]$
- Standard result. The $i\pi$ from $\ln(-1)$ when continuing $\ln(E - \hbar^2q^2/2m)$ past the pole. Correct.

**RG-D1.2 (beta function):** $\beta(g_R) = \frac{m}{\pi\hbar^2} g_R^2$
- From $\mu d(1/g_R)/d\mu = -m/(\pi\hbar^2)$ and $d(1/g)/d\mu = -g^{-2} dg/d\mu$. Correct.

**RG-D1.2a (shell integration):** $I_{\text{shell}}(0; \Lambda', \Lambda) = -\frac{m}{\pi\hbar^2}\ln(\Lambda/\Lambda')$
- At $E=0$: $\frac{1}{2\pi}\int_{\Lambda'}^{\Lambda} q\,dq \cdot (-2m/\hbar^2 q^2) = -\frac{m}{\pi\hbar^2}\ln(\Lambda/\Lambda')$. Correct.

**RG-P1.6 (dimensional analysis):** $[g] = \text{length}^{d-2}$
- $[\delta^{(d)}] = L^{-d}$, $[H] = E = L^{-2}$ (natural units), so $[g] = L^{-2}/L^{-d} = L^{d-2}$. Correct.

**RG-D1.6b (Robin scaling):** $\beta_{\hat\lambda} = -\hat\lambda$
- $[\lambda] = L^{-1}$, $\hat\lambda = \lambda/\mu$, $\mu d\hat\lambda/d\mu = -\hat\lambda$ (engineering). Correct.

**3D loop integral (Section 5.8.1):** $I_{3D} = -\frac{m}{\pi^2\hbar^2}\Lambda - i\frac{m}{2\pi\hbar^2}k + O(k^2/\Lambda)$
- Linear divergence in 3D: $\int_0^\Lambda q^2 dq / (k^2 - q^2) \sim -\Lambda$. The prefactor: $(2\pi)^{-3} \cdot 4\pi \cdot (-2m/\hbar^2) \cdot \Lambda = -m\Lambda/(\pi^2\hbar^2)$. Correct.

## Verdict

The interpretive framing ("RG is definitional," "two-layer QFT," "crown witnesses") continues the overclaiming pattern identified in the cross-paper diagnostic (notebooks/cross-paper-overclaiming-diagnostic.md). The Noether crown-witness issue is a direct repeat of the rcp-foundations error (S2.3 = Task #19 S2.1).
