# S² Half-Density Ordering Witness (Proposition D9.1h)

## §1. Setup

Manifold: \(S^2\) (unit sphere), coords \((\theta,\phi)\in(0,\pi)\times[0,2\pi)\).
Metric: \(g=d\theta^2+\sin^2\!\theta\,d\phi^2\), \(|g|=\sin^2\theta\).

Three operators on \(L^2(d\theta\,d\phi)\):

**(a) Left-ordered** (principal symbol only):
\[
H_L=-\partial_\theta^2-\frac{1}{\sin^2\!\theta}\,\partial_\phi^2.
\]

**(b) Laplace–Beltrami** (on \(L^2(\sin\theta\,d\theta\,d\phi)\)):
\[
-\Delta_g=-\partial_\theta^2-\cot\theta\,\partial_\theta-\frac{1}{\sin^2\!\theta}\,\partial_\phi^2.
\]

**(c) Half-density conjugated** (on \(L^2(d\theta\,d\phi)\)):
\[
\widetilde{H}:=(\sin\theta)^{1/2}\,(-\Delta_g)\,(\sin\theta)^{-1/2}
=H_L+V_{\mathrm{HD}}
\]
where
\[
V_{\mathrm{HD}}(\theta)=-\frac{1}{4}-\frac{1}{4\sin^2\!\theta}.
\]
